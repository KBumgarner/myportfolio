About Me

I'm an educational content specialist with a decade of experience in assessment development.

I've had the privilege of working as a key contributor to the North Carolina End-of-Grade (EOG) Reading tests for grades 6, 7, and 8. I am passionate about creating innovative and equitable learning experiences.

My professional mission is to leverage technology and innovative practices to make education more accessible, engaging, and impactful for all learners.
